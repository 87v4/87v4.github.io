Discord Server Rules

  1. Be Respectful – Treat all members with respect. No harassment, hate speech, or bullying.
  2. No Scamming – Scamming or fraudulent behavior is strictly prohibited. Verified trades only.
  3. No Self-Promotion Outside Channels – Do not advertise other servers or personal services unless allowed in the designated channels.
  4. Use Correct Channels – Keep trades and sales in the proper channels. Off-topic posts will be removed.
  5. Follow Discord’s Terms – All activity must comply with Discord’s Terms of Service. Selling illegal accounts or items is forbidden.
  6. Price Transparency – Clearly state the price of items or accounts. Misleading pricing is not allowed.
  7. No Spamming – Avoid excessive messages, repeated posts, or mass tagging.
  8. Respect Privacy – Do not share personal information of other members.
  9. Disputes and Moderation – If a dispute occurs, contact a moderator. Avoid public arguments in the server.
  10. Age Restriction – You must be 13+ to join and participate (Discord requirement).
  11. Verified Trades Encouraged – Consider using middleman services or verification to ensure safe transactions.
  12. Report Suspicious Activity – Notify moderators immediately if you suspect a scam or rule violation.
